Overview

That is the first thing I think of when I open the Urban Linker website design. The modular rectangle and square shapes really keep the whole website parallel and segmental. The text font used in the website really strikes a casual yet solemn tone. Many of the photographs used on the website are aesthetically pleasing and attractive to the eye which really catches the attention of the audience members. One interesting thing is that when you scroll over items on the website page, it interacts by either moving or something new slides over which makes the audience want to play around with the website. The usability is very efficient and the way the website layout is designed really persuades the audience to explore around.
