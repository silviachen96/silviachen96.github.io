Silvia Chen

My name is Silvia Chen and I am currently a third year design major at the University of California, Davis. I have always loved art since I was a little girl and enjoy using my imagination to create pieces. Not only is drawing a passion of mine, but I want to utilize my passion and take it to the next level by creating graphic designs. With my art and graphic designs, I want to benefit companies that focus on PR, planning events, and marketing. Whether it's creating birthday cards, making wedding invitations, designing Snapchat filters, I want my work to be inspiring to everybody who sees it! This portfolio features only some of my artwork and designs. HAPPY EXPLORING!
